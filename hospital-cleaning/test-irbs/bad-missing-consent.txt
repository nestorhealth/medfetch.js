RESEARCH PROTOCOL: Mental Health Outcomes Study

PRINCIPAL INVESTIGATOR: Dr. Emily Rodriguez, MD
Department of Psychiatry, University Hospital

STUDY TITLE: Effectiveness of Antidepressant Medications

STUDY OBJECTIVES:
This study aims to evaluate the effectiveness of different antidepressant medications in treating major depressive disorder.

TARGET POPULATION:
- Patients aged 18-65 years
- Diagnosed with major depressive disorder
- Both male and female participants

DATA REQUIREMENTS:
- Patient demographics
- Depression severity scores
- Medication history
- Treatment outcomes

RESOURCE TYPES NEEDED:
- Patient: Basic demographics
- Condition: Depression diagnoses
- Observation: Depression scores
- MedicationRequest: Antidepressant medications
- Encounter: Clinical visits

FILTERS AND CRITERIA:
- Age range: 18-65 years
- Conditions: Major depressive disorder
- Date range: 2020-01-01 to 2025-12-31
- Target patient count: 200 patients

DATA SECURITY:
- Data will be stored securely
- Access limited to research team

MONITORING:
- Regular review of study progress
